About

Bray Media

Bray Media, LLC, is a company operating in the edTech industry. In particular, the firm will work in the specific market segment of the minority representation media content generation and provision. In detail, Bray Media is a media platform that will be dedicated to the provision of diverse young business professionals and minority underrepresented community youth. Bray Media represents them with media content that brings awareness to everything diversity and inclusion by providing industry insights everything corporations and society are doing to create a more diverse-driven workforce.

Bray Media is an access -point for digital content for young minority business professionals. The platform will provide users with media content (Video Podcast, Podcast, Masterclass presentations, blog forums, and career opportunity ) to access first-hand introductions from influential business professionals. The latter inspires to motivate, educate, and inspire youth to create a more diverse workforce.
